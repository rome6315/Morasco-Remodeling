About Us

Morasco Remodeling is owned and operated by Perrin Morasco, a Pittsburgh native with roots in North Huntingdon, North Versailles, Penn Hills, Apollo, Bloomfield and Lawrenceville.

A message from Perrin: I learned the trade from my grandfather starting at an early age before embarking on a career in Graphic Design in the early 90’s. During my 20 year tenure as a designer, I continued to work in the remodeling field in my spare time, helping friends, relatives and neighbors with their projects. I found that my expertise in the Graphic Design field lent itself well to the remodeling/decorating industry giving me a unique perspective when drafting up plans for a remodeling job. In 2011 I decided to take on remodeling as a full time career because of my love of the craft and the satisfaction that comes from seeing my customers happy. Going above and beyond to gain their trust is as equally important to me. Most of my customers I now consider my friends, and they are free to contact me when the have any questions or just need advice on a repair or project they are attempting to do on their own. I look forward to serving your needs. Don’t hesitate to give me a call or shoot me a text. I'll do whatever I can to solve your problem or meet your needs, and if it’s a field I don’t practice, I can recommend someone who can help you.


-Perrin J Morasco
